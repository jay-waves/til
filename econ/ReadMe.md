## 曼昆的经济学十大原理

经济学研究如何控制稀缺性，以最大效率进行资源配置。
1. people face trade-off.
2. The cost of something is what you give up to get it.
3. Rational people think at the margin.
4. people respond to incentives
5. Tarde can make everyone better off
6. Markets are usually a good way to organize ecnonomic activity.
7. Governments can sometimes improve market outcomes 
8. A country's standard of living depends on its ability to produce goods and services.
9. Prices rise when the government prints too much monye.
10. Society faces a short-run trade-off between inflation and unemployment.
